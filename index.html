About us

We are team comprised of pharmacists, technicians and patient care coordinators specially trained.

We will help you every step of the way from insurance authorizations and co- pays to enrolling you in funding assistance program. Our expert technicians help at navigating through financial paperwork and finding ways to help ensure your medications are available.

We offer support by:

  • Helping you manage side effects and reminding you when it's time for a refill.
  • Confirming your dosage and medication schedule.
  • Educating you on how and when to take your medication.
  • Providing drug counseling and making sure you receive your medication in time.
  • Answering your questions.

Our services
